Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines the privacy practices for the Infinite Adventure mobile application (the "App"), available on the Google Play Store.

This policy is to inform you that Infinite Adventure is designed with user privacy as a top priority.

1. No Data Collection

Infinite Adventure does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. We do not gather any information that can be used to identify you or your device. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other contact information.
  • Usernames, passwords, or other account credentials.
  • Device identifiers (e.g., IMEI, MAC address, advertising ID).
  • Usage data, analytics, or app interaction information that is sent off the device.
  • Any other information that could be considered personal or sensitive.

Since we do not collect any user data, there is no data to use, share, or sell to third parties.

2. Location Data

Infinite Adventure does not collect, use, or share your precise or approximate location. The App does not require access to your device's location services to function.

3. Special Permissions

Infinite Adventure requires no special permissions to run on your device. The App is designed to operate without needing access to sensitive device features or data, such as your contacts, camera, microphone, storage, or other areas that often require explicit user consent. The standard permissions automatically granted by the Android operating system for any app to run are utilized, but no additional, potentially privacy-intrusive permissions are requested or used.

4. Data Safety Section on Google Play Store

The information provided in the Data Safety section of our Google Play Store listing accurately reflects these practices, indicating that no user data is collected or shared by the App.

5. Third-Party Services

Infinite Adventure does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect data from users on our behalf.

6. Children's Privacy

Since Infinite Adventure does not collect any user data, it does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has inadvertently provided any information, please contact us, though we reiterate that our App is not designed to collect such information.
